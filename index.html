  • Info

    Medicinal leaves refer to plant foliage with therapeutic properties, utilized across cultures for healing purposes.

    These leaves contain bioactive compounds like antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, offering diverse health benefits.

    Commonly employed in traditional medicine systems such as Ayurveda, Chinese medicine, and Indigenous practices, medicinal leaves are brewed into teas, used topically as poultices, or consumed raw.
